About Us

This project was created by 4 students from the University of Massachusetts Boston: Gaius Dukuly, Dana Cheng, Birti Gebregziher and Siraad Mohamud. It was assigned by Simon Tran who is the professor of the IT 485 capstone class. The project began on February and was completed over a 4 months period. Gaius and Birti was responsible for the front-end of the application and Dana and Siraad for the back-end. HTML&CSS was ulitize for the front-end to display and design the features of the application and Python and SQLalchemy to apply those features (Internal and External APIs) using the data collected from the users.
